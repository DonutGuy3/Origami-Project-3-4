Some intresting facts about the camel

Some intresting facts about the chameleon

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  2. Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  3. Most chameleons have prehensile tails that they use to wrap around the tree branches

Some intresting facts about the pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals

  3. Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two chicks at the same time
